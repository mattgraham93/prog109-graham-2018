Mental health and data go hand-in-hand

I recently had a realization on what I want to do to help leave a positive impact on this world
As someone who suffers from anxiety and depression and feels a deep empathy for humanity, it has become my goal to help leave a positive impact on this world through teaching the importance of mental health backed with data.
My goal is to start by gathering data and modelling what would change locally, nationally, and hopefully internationally. I believe that bringing awareness to everyone's mental health can have a positive economic and cultural impact. To start, this theme will be derived from colors that are particularly pleasing to those who suffer from anxiety and depression. These colors are: turquoise, violet, and green. These colors will be on a back background and will be common throughout.
